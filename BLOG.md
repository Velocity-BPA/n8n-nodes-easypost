# Streamline Your Shipping Operations: Introducing the n8n EasyPost Node

We're excited to announce the release of our latest community node for n8n: the EasyPost integration! Developed by Velocity BPA, this node brings powerful multi-carrier shipping capabilities directly into your n8n workflows.

## The Shipping Automation Challenge

If you've ever managed e-commerce operations, fulfillment processes, or logistics workflows, you know the pain of juggling multiple shipping carriers, manually generating labels, and keeping customers updated with tracking information. Address verification errors can lead to failed deliveries, and comparing rates across carriers is time-consuming and error-prone.

The EasyPost node for n8n eliminates these headaches by connecting your workflows to EasyPost's comprehensive shipping API, giving you access to USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL, and dozens of other carriers through a single, unified interface.

## Key Features

Our EasyPost node empowers you to:

- **Generate shipping labels** automatically when orders are received
- **Compare rates** across multiple carriers in real-time to find the best price
- **Verify addresses** before shipping to reduce failed deliveries
- **Track shipments** and trigger notifications when packages are delivered
- **Purchase postage** and manage shipping insurance programmatically
- **Integrate with your existing tools** like Shopify, WooCommerce, or custom e-commerce platforms

Whether you're running a small online store or managing enterprise-level fulfillment operations, this node scales with your needs.

## Getting Started

Installing the EasyPost node is simple. In your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-easypost


After installation, restart n8n, and you'll find the EasyPost node available in your node palette. You'll need an EasyPost API key to get started, which you can obtain by signing up for a free account at EasyPost.com.
